Your friend in the legal business

Dealing with legal issues can be complicated and stressful. DigitalLawyer makes it easier by guiding you along the way.

How does it work?

Here's what you can expect when you use our service to get assistance with a personal or business-related legal issue.

1. Intake interview

You'll connect with a legal concierge who will perform a full intake of your legal issue. You'll receive the results of this intake interview in writing, so you can use it as a reference when speaking with attorneys about your case.

2. Attorney introductions

We'll use the information collected in the intake interview to identify attorneys and other legal professionals who can help. We take account of your specific needs and budget, and help you identify options.

3. Case report

You'll receive a case report containing helpful information and resources, and price information pertaining to your particular legal issue.

4. Check In

We check in with you to make sure that you were able to connect with one or more attorneys, and to help you keep things on track, from intake to outcome.

How much does it cost?

The Legal Concierge Service starts at $149 per legal issue. We're confident it will help you save thousands.

Can you help with any legal issue?

While our services cover a broad range of specialties and jurisdictions, we might not always be able to assist. In such a case, we'll be transparent, and will refund any fees.

Will you act as my attorney?

No. Your legal concierge can help you understand your options, but cannot give you legal advice and is not your attorney.
